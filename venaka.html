Warframe

Warframe is a free-to-play online action game developed and published by Digital Extremes.
The game features a blend of third-person shooter and melee combat elements, set within a sci-fi universe. Players assume the role of the Tenno, ancient warriors who have awoken from centuries of cryosleep to find themselves at war with different factions across the solar system.
