In the game Warframe, the Grineer are a major antagonistic faction known for their militaristic and aggressive nature.

The Grineer rely heavily on cloning technology to maintain their numbers, but this process has led to severe genetic decay.

n Warframe, the Corpus is one of the main enemy factions, characterized by their reliance on advanced technology and commercial motives.

The Corpus are known for their advanced technology and robotics. They manufacture and deploy a wide variety of robotic units

Infested

In Warframe, the Infested are one of the most dangerous and grotesque factions players encounter. They represent a nightmarish blend of biology and technology, resulting from a rampant techno-organic virus that corrupts and assimilates any living or robotic matter it comes into contact with.

The Infested are the result of the Technocyte Virus, an Orokin-created bio-weapon designed to assimilate and repurpose biological matter. This virus turns living beings and machines into grotesque, mindless abominations that act as a single, ravenous hive mind

Infested units are horrifying amalgamations of flesh and metal, often featuring multiple limbs, exposed musculature, and other disturbing features. Common Infested enemies include Chargers, Runners, and Ancients, each with unique abilities and attack patterns.
